The Label

About Us

Our Philosophy

Sound Without
Compromise

Singh Sound Syndicate was founded on a single conviction: the best music doesn't fit neatly into boxes. We are an artist-first, genre-agnostic independent label built to support creators who refuse to water down their vision for commercial convenience.

From the yé-yé pop revival of the long-lost music of the wife and husband duo Coquine to the desert thunder of Sons of the Golden Age, our roster spans decades of influence and every corner of the sonic spectrum. What unites them isn't genre — it's integrity. Every artist on our slate has something urgent to say, and we give them the platform, resources, and creative freedom to say it on their own terms.

We handle global distribution, sync licensing, and brand partnerships — but we never dictate the art. The music comes first. Always.

Our Story

From a Basement Studio
to the World Stage

What started as tape recordings in the 1980s has grown into one of the most distinctive independent imprints operating today. The pandemic forced us inward — and we used that time to build something real.

Our name pays homage to heritage and community. "Singh" means lion and represents courage; "Sound" is our medium; "Syndicate" speaks to collective power. Together, these words embody everything we stand for: fearless music that lifts each other up.

Our Values
