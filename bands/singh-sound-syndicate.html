Biography

The Story

Singh Sound Syndicate is the boundless soundscape of M. Chana, an eclectic multi-instrumental artist whose East-West roots ignite a music that defies borders — blending blues, jazz, rock, country, folk, Indian rhythms, and more into a sound that's raw, vibrant, and untamed. From a Punjabi heart to global stages, Chana crafts songs that pulse with life.

Bluesingh, dropped May 2025, was the first chapter — a debut that kicked off a multi-genre journey. Singh Sound Syndicate is ever-changing tapestry of musicians and music under the guiding hand of its creator. With multiple albums already released and more charting new sonic terrain, this is music for those who crave the unexpected.
