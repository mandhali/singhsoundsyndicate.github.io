Biography

The Story

Neon Chai is the greatest '80s band of the 21st century with authentic grooves that harken back to a better time. Analogue synthesizers, driving beats, and vocals that drip with attitude. The four-piece band blends modern musical motifs with the glossy sheen of '80s new wave, creating something that's both a party starter and a cultural statement.

Neon Chai consists of band members Chana - Bass, Lead Vocals; Elowen Fable - Keyboards, Lead Vocals; Yze Yildiz - Percussion, Vocals; Xun Yong - Lead Guitar, Vocals. The Greatest '80s act of the 21st century.
