Biography

The Story

Red River Creek makes music that sounds like it was born on a long stretch of empty highway — wide open, slightly dangerous, and impossibly beautiful. The four-piece band draws from country, blues, and heartland rock to create an Americana sound that's rooted in tradition but refuses to be nostalgic.

Red River Creek are vocalists and musicians Lee Monroe, Jasper Harlan, Caleb Whitaker, and Chana Singh. New music is coming — watch this space.
