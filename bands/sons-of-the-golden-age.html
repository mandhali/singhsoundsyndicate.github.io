Biography

The Story

Sons of the Golden Age channel the raw, mythic power of '70s rock through a modern lens. With thundering riffs that echo across desert canyons, a rhythm section that hits like tectonic plates shifting, and vocals that range from whispered menace to full-throated roar, they've become the torchbearers of a classic sound that refuses to die.
