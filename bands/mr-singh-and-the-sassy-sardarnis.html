Biography

The Story

Mr. Singh and the Sassy Sardarnis fire off comedy and satire that cuts deep and cracks you up. From country twang to ska struts to disco-rap beats to soul and rock 'n roll, they roast the ridiculous — woke trends, globalist suits, anything begging for a jab.

Mr. Singh's sharp pen and the Sardarnis' fierce vibe bring Sikh swagger to every track, hopping genres but never losing the funny. Think barnyard anthems one day, dancefloor burns the next, all with zero apologies. Your playlist deserves this rebellion — stream, laugh, repeat.
