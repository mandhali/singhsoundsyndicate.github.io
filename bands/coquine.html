Biography

The Story

Paris, 1965–1966 In a city that judged them, Mallory Coquine and Taj Sian found each other. Over four months of stolen nights, they secretly married and recorded an album that fused yé-yé pop with the soul of the sitar. They performed only four underground shows. On Valentine’s Day 1966, their story ended in a fiery crash.

The album Mille Bisous — 13 tracks of passion, rebellion, and love — vanished with them, buried by feuding families for sixty years. Now, it has been unearthed. A thousand kisses, finally heard.
